UNICEF is hiring work-from-home IT professionals to join our team and help us build a better world for every child.

We are looking for talented and motivated individuals with a passion for making a difference in the lives of children. As a work-from-home UNICEF IT professional, you will have the opportunity to use your skills and experience to support our global mission of protecting the rights of every child.

To be eligible for a work-from-home IT job at UNICEF, you must:

  • Have a bachelor's degree in computer science or a related field
  • Have at least 3 years of experience in a relevant IT role
  • Be fluent in English
  • Be able to work independently and as part of a team
